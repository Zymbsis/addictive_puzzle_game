FAQ

  • How do I reset a level?

    Tap the reset button to start over.

  • Can I skip a level?

    Yes, you can use hints to help solve or skip.

  • Are there in-app purchases?

    Yes, you can buy hints and other perks.

  • Is it suitable for children?

    Absolutely, it enhances problem-solving skills.

  • How many levels are there?

    Over 500 levels with more added regularly.

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes, the game is fully playable without an internet connection.
